Last updated: September 2025

1. Acceptance of Terms

By using the SAL College Lost & Found System, you agree to be bound by these Terms and Conditions.

2. User Responsibilities

  • You must be a current student of SAL College to use this system
  • Provide accurate information when reporting lost or found items
  • Respect the privacy and property of others
  • Use the system only for legitimate lost and found purposes

3. Item Reporting

When reporting found items, you must provide accurate descriptions and contact information. For lost items, provide truthful information to help facilitate returns.

4. Privacy

Your personal information will be used solely for the purpose of facilitating lost and found item returns and will not be shared with third parties.

5. Prohibited Activities

  • False reporting of items
  • Harassment of other users
  • Commercial use of the system
  • Any illegal activities

6. Limitation of Liability

SAL College is not responsible for any lost, damaged, or unreturned items. The system serves as a platform to connect finders and owners.
